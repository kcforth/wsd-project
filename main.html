Amish Beef Stew

Serve this hearty meal and warm them right to the toes.

You will need...

  1. 3 cups cubed beef
  2. 1 med. onion, cubed
  3. 8 lg. potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
  4. 2 cups carrots, sliced
  5. 2 cups celery, sliced
  6. 1 cup tomato juice
  7. 1/2 cup flour
  8. 1/4 cup margarine
  9. 1/2 cup frozen peas

In a hot skillet with the margarine, brown the beef in onions and flour. Transfer the cooked beef and onions into the slow cooker. Heat on low for two hours, then adding the remaining ingredients. Cook for 2 more hours and serve.
